About Us

Who We Are

We started as a group of classical musicians frustrated with the existing way of finding and obtaining fine string instruments. We started this service to make string instruments more accessible.

What We Do

Our team is always on the lookout for new fine instruments globally. When we find one that meets our criteria, we share it with you here so that you have the best options when selecting your next instrument.

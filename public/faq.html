Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the trial period for an instrument?

A: Instruments are big purchases. As such, we allow for one month trials for each instrument to let you know for sure if it's the right fit.

Q: How does shipping work?

A: We pay for outbound shipping and you pay for return shipping should you not choose the instrument. All instruments are insured.

Q: How often do new instruments come in?

A: It varies, but we typically find six to ten instruments that meet our standards every month.
